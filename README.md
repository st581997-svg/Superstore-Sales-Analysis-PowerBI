## 🚀 Key Business Insights
Based on the visual analysis of the dashboard:
* **Immediate Alerts:** A critical **96.2% dip in Sales** and a **96.9% dip in Profit** were identified compared to the Previous Month (PMTD), signaling a need for immediate operational review.
* **Customer Dominance:** The **Consumer Segment** represents the majority of the business, driving **51.63%** of total order volume.
* **Seasonal Trends:** Significant sales peaks occur in **November ($352.5K)** and **September ($307.6K)**, suggesting high impact from Q3 and Q4 seasonal promotions.
* **Product Performance:** **Phones** and **Chairs** are the leading sub-categories, contributing the highest revenue within the "Category Vase Sales" analysis.

## Advanced DAX Measures
I developed custom DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) to drive dynamic reporting:
* **MTD vs. YTD Switching:** Created a calculation group/toggle to allow users to flip between Month-to-Date and Year-to-Date views seamlessly.
* **Comparative Metrics:** Calculated `% Variance vs. PMTD` to provide context to the raw Sales and Profit numbers.
* **AOV Tracking:** Developed an **Average Order Value (AOV)** measure to monitor transaction quality over time.

  ### UI/UX Design Principles
* **Executive Layout:** Followed a "Top-Down" hierarchy, placing the most critical KPIs (Customers, Orders, Sales, Profit) at the very top for instant absorption.
* **High-Contrast Theme:** Used a dark-themed UI to reduce eye strain and make the colorful data indicators (Red/Green/Blue) stand out.
* **Interactivity:** Integrated **Slicers for Year (2014-2017)** and **Segment** to allow for multi-dimensional data exploration.
